Poltician Voting Classifier

We takes past bills and clustering them based on the topics they contain. We take those clusters and attached them to a politicains voting record. From this we trained a support vector classifier (SVC) to predict how they are going to vote on a test bill. This model takes into account voting records, social media, and socioeconomic information.
